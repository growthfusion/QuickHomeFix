1. Who are we?

QuickHomeFix is operated by Sharp Ads Inc, doing business as Growth Fusion. QuickHomeFix is a platform that connects homeowners with home improvement service professionals. We are not a contractor or service provider ourselves.

This Privacy Notice applies to all information collected through our website and any related services, communications, or interactions.

Our services are intended for users who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

2. What categories of data does QuickHomeFix collect?

We collect several categories of personal information:

Identity data

Your first name and last name.

Location details

Your physical address, city, state, and zip code.

Contact details

Your email address and phone number.

Project information

Details about the home improvement services you are interested in.

Technical information

IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, pages viewed, referring URL, date and time of access.

3. We need your consent to this Privacy Policy

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal data as described herein.

When you submit your information, you expressly consent to being contacted by QuickHomeFix and up to four home improvement companies via phone calls, text messages, and emails.

4. What rights do you have?

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Right of access: Request information about what data we hold.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit our use of your data.
  • Right to data portability: Request a copy in a structured format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing for direct marketing.

Contact us at support@quickhomefix.pro to exercise these rights.

5. Special Notice for California Residents

California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA and CPRA, including the right to know, delete, correct, opt out, and not receive discriminatory treatment.

QuickHomeFix does not sell your personal information. To submit a request, email support@quickhomefix.pro.

7. How does QuickHomeFix receive your Personal Data?

Information you provide directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out forms, request quotes, or communicate with us.

Information collected automatically

We may automatically collect technical data including IP address, browser type, and user interactions. We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies.

8. How does QuickHomeFix use your Personal Data?

  • Service matching: To connect you with professionals based on your needs.
  • Lead delivery: To deliver your information to partners.
  • Communications: To respond to inquiries and contact you about your project.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve our website.
  • Security: To protect our services and prevent fraud.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with legal obligations.

9. How do we share your Personal Data?

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service partners when you request quotes.

  • Legal requirements when required by law.
  • Business transfers in connection with mergers or acquisitions.

10. How do we secure your Personal Data?

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission is completely secure.

11. International transfers

Our services are primarily directed to users in the United States. If you access from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
